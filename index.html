We archive and support data sets applicable to several NASA Earth Science Focus Areas including: Atmospheric Composition, Water & Energy Cycles, and Climate Variability in addition to data sets applicable to Carbon Cycle & Ecosystem. Currently we have over 1500 data collections being archived and distributed and utilized publicly for a wide range of research and applications. Many of these research efforts result in peer reviewed publications that are published in various scattered leading world scientific, computational and engineering journals. To better promote our data and services to our users, it is useful to create and maintain a catalog of peer reviewed publications that relate to a given data product that we archive. Having such a local catalog system then enables us to present our users with targeted or more relevant publications that can significantly contribute to their research efforts.
